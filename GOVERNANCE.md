# Governance

## Project model

Future Risk Radar is maintainer-led with community contributions.

## Roles

- **Maintainers**: steward roadmap, review/merge PRs, manage releases, enforce policies.
- **Contributors**: submit issues, docs, tests, and code improvements.

## Decision making

- Changes are discussed in issues/PRs.
- Maintainers seek rough consensus.
- If consensus cannot be reached, maintainers make the final decision.

## Becoming a maintainer

Regular contributors demonstrating sustained, high-quality contribution and constructive collaboration may be invited by maintainers.
